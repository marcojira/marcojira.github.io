Papers

Regret Dynamics in Online Clustering | Paper, Poster
Marco Jiralerspong*, Andjela Mladenovic* and Gauthier Gidel

Analysis of regret dynamics of an online Follow-The-Leader (FTL) k-means algorithm and an online FTL Gaussian Mixture Model (GMM).

Comparison of Metrics Used in Generative Adversarial Networks | Paper

Course project for COMP 598 (Mathematical Foundations for Machine Learning) comparing various metrics used for the training of GANs.

Neural Network Models for Inflation Rate Forecasting | Paper

Course project for ECON 420 testing the performance of MLPs, RNNs and CNNs for inflation rate forecasting from a relatively large macroeconomic dataset.
